Mariela

CGBP Lead Designer

Mariela received her degree in architecture from the University of Buenos Aires, Argentina in 2003, where she holds a professional license. After completing her studies, she partnered with Estudio Maraude, working on a variety of residential and commercial projects until she moved to California in 2004, where she joined Square Three Design Studios and later, Bellomo Architects, in Palo Alto, CA as a project designer. Her interest in urban design and environmentally responsible architecture inspired her to participate in a few local and international competitions for which she has received honorable mentions & awards. Mariela has been collaborating with 360 design studio since its inception and officially joined the firm at the beginning of 2008. Born in Argentina and growing up in Germany, she brings with her a rich and diverse background, a strong technical expertise and a lifelong passion for modern architecture.
