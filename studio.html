Our Philosophy

At 360 design studio our approach to design is driven by a sense of responsibility we feel for meeting our client's needs while serving our community and the environment. For every project we work on, we hope that our intervention in the field of architecture leaves a positive impact on our surroundings. We start the design process by spending time with our client to ensure we have a deep understanding of their needs and the context into which they're building. Our architectural services include but are not limited to: programming, site analysis, schematic design, design development, construction documentation and permits, bidding and negotiations and construction administration.
