Bahi

Architect - Principal

Bahi graduated with a Bachelors and a Masters degree in architecture from University of Houston in Texas in 1993. In her last year of undergraduate studies, she was selected to study architectural and Urbanism in France. During this year and in the years that followed, she traveled throughout Europe and the middle-east. Bahi began her professional career overseas where she participated in award-winning national competitions and cultural projects. She later joined "NBBJ" and "Callison Architecture" in Seattle, WA as a project designer working on retail and hospitality projects serving world re-known clients. In 1998, she joined Square three design studios in Palo Alto, CA managing award winning custom residential projects. Bahi started 360 design studios in 2004. Her strong technical background, her diverse experience, her passion for architecture and her love of working with people has helped make 360 design studio into a successful and steadily growing business serving clients throughout the SF/Bay area.
